# LinkedIn Setup

Connect your LinkedIn profile or company page to share professional content directly from GenFeed.ai.

## Prerequisites

- LinkedIn account (personal or company page)
- Admin access for company pages
- Profile completeness (improves reach)
- Active account status

## Account Types

### Personal Profile

- Share as individual professional
- Build thought leadership
- Network expansion
- Personal branding

### Company Page

- Represent your organization
- Share company updates
- Recruit talent
- Build brand awareness

## Connection Process

### Connecting Your Account

1. Navigate to **Accounts** â†’ **Add Account**
2. Select **LinkedIn**
3. Choose account type:
   - Personal Profile
   - Company Page
4. Click **Connect with LinkedIn**
5. Log in to LinkedIn
6. Review and accept permissions:
   - Share content
   - Access profile info
   - View analytics
   - Manage company pages (if applicable)

### Multiple Accounts

- Connect multiple profiles
- Manage company pages
- Switch between accounts
- Team member access

## Content Types

### Text Posts

- **Length**: Up to 3,000 characters
- **Best Practice**: 150-300 characters
- **Formatting**: Bold, italic, lists
- **Mentions**: Tag people and companies
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 recommended

### Image Posts

- **Formats**: JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Size**: Max 10MB
- **Dimensions**: 1200x627px recommended
- **Multiple Images**: Up to 9 images
- **Alt Text**: Accessibility feature

### Video Posts

- **Duration**: 10 seconds to 10 minutes
- **Size**: Max 5GB
- **Format**: MP4 recommended
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - Square: 1:1
  - Landscape: 16:9
  - Vertical: 9:16
- **Captions**: Auto-generated available

### Articles

- **Length**: Unlimited
- **Formatting**: Rich text editor
- **Media**: Embed images, videos
- **SEO**: Google indexed
- **Analytics**: Detailed metrics

### Documents

- **Formats**: PDF, PPT, Word
- **Size**: Max 100MB
- **Pages**: Up to 300
- **Preview**: In-feed viewing
- **Downloads**: Track engagement

## Publishing Features

### Content Creation

1. Create content in GenFeed.ai
2. Select **Publish to LinkedIn**
3. Choose post type
4. Customize for LinkedIn:
   - Professional tone
   - Industry keywords
   - Relevant hashtags
5. Add media if needed
6. Schedule or publish

### LinkedIn-Specific Tools

- **Professional Tone Checker**: Ensure appropriate language
- **Industry Hashtags**: Relevant tag suggestions
- **Connection Tagger**: Mention your network
- **Company Page Selector**: Choose posting account
- **Employee Advocacy**: Share to team members

### Scheduling Options

- Optimal time suggestions
- Time zone management
- Recurring posts
- Content calendar view
- Bulk scheduling

## Best Practices

### Optimal Posting Times

- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Best engagement
- **Morning**: 7:30-9:00 AM
- **Lunch**: 12:00-1:00 PM
- **Evening**: 5:00-6:00 PM
- **Time Zones**: Consider global audience

### Content Strategy

1. **Value-First**: Share insights, not just promotions
2. **Storytelling**: Personal experiences resonate
3. **Industry News**: Comment on trends
4. **Questions**: Encourage discussions
5. **Visual Content**: 2x more engagement

### Professional Tone

- Avoid excessive emojis
- Use proper grammar
- Share credible sources
- Maintain authenticity
- Respect diverse viewpoints

## LinkedIn Algorithm

### Key Factors

1. **Dwell Time**: How long people read
2. **Early Engagement**: First hour crucial
3. **Comments**: Valued over likes
4. **Creator Authority**: Industry expertise
5. **Content Quality**: Original insights

### Visibility Boosters

- Engage with comments quickly
- Tag relevant people (sparingly)
- Use native video
- Share during peak hours
- Create conversation starters

### Article Metrics

- **Traffic Sources**: Discovery methods
- **Comments**: Engagement quality

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Connection Failed**

- Verify LinkedIn login
- Check account permissions
- Ensure active account
- Try incognito mode

**Posting Errors**

- Check content length
- Verify media formats
- Review for restricted content
- Check posting frequency

**Limited Reach**

- Improve content quality
- Post at optimal times
- Engage with network
- Use relevant hashtags

**Analytics Not Showing**

- Wait 24-48 hours
- Check privacy settings
- Verify page admin access
- Clear browser cache

### API Limitations

- Rate limits apply
- 100 shares per day
- 1000 API calls daily
- Respect throttling
