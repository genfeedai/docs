# Getting Started with GenFeed.ai

Welcome to GenFeed.ai! This guide will walk you through the basics of using our platform to generate AI-powered content for your business.

## Quick Start

### 1. Sign Up and Login

1. Visit [genfeed.ai](https://genfeed.ai) and click "Sign Up"
2. Create your account using your email or social login
3. Choose your subscription plan based on your needs

### 2. Understanding the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with several key areas:

- **Studio**: Your creative workspace for generating content
- **Assets Library**: Store and manage your generated images, videos, and audio
- **Accounts**: Manage your social media accounts and publishing settings
- **Analytics**: Track your content performance and usage metrics
- **Settings**: Configure your preferences and team settings

### 3. Your First Generation

#### Generate an Image

1. Navigate to **Studio** → **Image Generation**
2. Choose your preferred AI model (e.g., Leonardo AI, Google Imagen 4)
3. Enter your prompt describing the image you want
4. Click **Generate** and wait for your image

#### Generate a Video

1. Go to **Studio** → **Video Generation**
2. Select a video model (e.g., Kling AI, Google Veo 3)
3. Describe the video scene you want to create
4. Set duration and other parameters
5. Click **Generate** to create your video

#### Generate Audio

1. Visit **Studio** → **Audio Generation**
2. Choose between:
   - **Voice**: Text-to-speech with ElevenLabs
   - **Music**: Generate background music with Meta MusicGen
3. Enter your text or music description
4. Generate your audio content

## Credit System

### Understanding Credits

- Each generation consumes credits based on the model used
- Premium models (GPT-4, Claude Opus) use more credits
- Check current credit balance in the top bar
- Credits reset monthly based on your subscription

### Credit Costs by Model Type

- **Text Models**: 1-20 credits per generation
- **Image Models**: 5-50 credits per image
- **Video Models**: 20-100 credits per video
- **Audio Models**: 10-30 credits per generation

### Common Issues

**Generation Failed**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have sufficient credits
- Try a different model or simplify your prompt

**Slow Generation**
- Premium models take longer but produce better results
- Video generation typically takes 1-5 minutes
- Use faster models for quick iterations

**Quality Issues**
- Be specific in your prompts
- Use style keywords and references
- Try different models for different results
