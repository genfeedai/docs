# Model Selection Guide

Choose the right AI model for your content to get the best results. This guide helps you understand when to use each model and how to optimize your prompts for each one.

## Overview

GenFeed offers multiple AI models for different content types. Each model has strengths and weaknesses. Choosing the right model and optimizing your prompt for that model will dramatically improve your results.

## Video Models

### Kling AI V2

**Best For:**

- Creative and artistic videos
- Stylized content
- Fluid motion and transitions
- Unique visual interpretations

**Strengths:**

- Excellent motion quality
- Artistic interpretation
- Creative visual effects
- Smooth transitions

**When to Use:**

- You want unique, eye-catching visuals
- Artistic or stylized content
- Creative projects
- Motion-heavy content

**Prompting Tips:**

- Emphasize style and mood
- Describe motion and movement
- Use artistic descriptors
- Include transition preferences

**Example:**

```
Create an artistic video of a dancer in a surreal landscape.
Style: Dreamy, ethereal with flowing movements.
Motion: Smooth, graceful dance movements.
Transitions: Soft dissolves between scenes.
Mood: Mystical and enchanting.
```

### Google Veo 3

**Best For:**

- Professional, high-quality content
- Cinematic videos
- Realistic rendering
- Commercial content

**Strengths:**

- Highest resolution output
- Photorealistic quality
- Professional appearance
- Excellent detail

**When to Use:**

- Quality is paramount
- Professional/commercial content
- Realistic visuals required
- High-resolution needs

**Prompting Tips:**

- Be specific about real-world details
- Include camera and lighting details
- Describe professional quality
- Specify resolution needs

**Example:**

```
Create a professional product showcase video.
Quality: Cinematic, 4K resolution.
Camera: Smooth tracking shots, professional lighting.
Style: Clean, modern, commercial aesthetic.
Details: Sharp focus, realistic materials and textures.
```

### Runway

**Best For:**

- Realistic video generation
- Natural motion
- High-quality output
- Versatile content types

**Strengths:**

- Realistic motion
- Good quality output
- Versatile applications
- Reliable results

**When to Use:**

- Need realistic video
- Versatile content needs
- Consistent quality
- Natural motion required

**Prompting Tips:**

- Focus on realistic details
- Describe natural motion
- Include environmental context
- Specify quality expectations

### Luma

**Best For:**

- High-quality video output
- Professional content
- Detailed visuals
- Consistent results

**Strengths:**

- High quality
- Good detail rendering
- Consistent output
- Professional appearance

**When to Use:**

- Need high-quality output
- Professional projects
- Detailed visuals important
- Consistent results needed

## Image Models

### Google Imagen 4

**Best For:**

- Photorealistic images
- Product photography
- Commercial imagery
- Real-world scenes

**Strengths:**

- Most photorealistic
- Excellent detail
- Professional quality
- Real-world accuracy

**When to Use:**

- Need photorealistic images
- Product photography
- Commercial content
- Real-world accuracy important

**Prompting Tips:**

- Include camera settings: "shot with 85mm lens, f/1.4"
- Specify real-world details
- Describe lighting conditions
- Include professional photography terms

**Example:**

```
Professional product photography of a luxury watch.
Camera: Shot with 85mm lens, f/1.4 aperture.
Lighting: Dramatic side lighting creating reflections.
Background: Pure white seamless backdrop.
Style: Commercial, high-end product photography.
Details: Sharp focus on craftsmanship, metallic reflections.
```

### Leonardo AI

**Best For:**

- Artistic and stylized images
- Creative content
- Unique visual styles
- Artistic interpretations

**Strengths:**

- Artistic excellence
- Style variety
- Creative interpretations
- Visual effects

**When to Use:**

- Want artistic/stylized content
- Creative projects
- Unique visual styles
- Artistic interpretation needed

**Prompting Tips:**

- Include style references: "in the style of [artist]"
- Use descriptive adjectives for textures
- Specify artistic style
- Include color palette preferences

**Example:**

```
Create an artistic portrait in the style of impressionist painting.
Style: Soft brushstrokes, dreamy atmosphere.
Colors: Warm pastels with golden hour lighting.
Mood: Serene and contemplative.
Texture: Visible brushwork, artistic interpretation.
```

### SDXL (Stable Diffusion XL)

**Best For:**

- Versatile image generation
- Various styles
- Good quality output
- Flexible applications

**Strengths:**

- Versatile
- Good quality
- Style flexibility
- Reliable results

**When to Use:**

- Need versatile image generation
- Various style needs
- Good quality sufficient
- Flexible applications

**Prompting Tips:**

- Be clear about desired style
- Include composition details
- Specify quality expectations
- Describe visual elements clearly

### DALL-E 3 (GPT Image)

**Best For:**

- Complex concepts
- Abstract ideas
- Editorial illustrations
- Text in images

**Strengths:**

- Understands complex concepts
- Handles abstract ideas
- Good for illustrations
- Can include text

**When to Use:**

- Complex or abstract concepts
- Editorial illustrations
- Need text in image
- Conceptual content

**Prompting Tips:**

- Describe concepts clearly
- Use metaphors and analogies
- Specify if text needed
- Include style preferences

## Model Selection Decision Tree

### For Video:

1. **Need highest quality?** → Google Veo 3
2. **Want artistic/stylized?** → Kling AI V2
3. **Need realistic?** → Runway or Luma
4. **Versatile needs?** → Runway

### For Image:

1. **Need photorealistic?** → Google Imagen 4
2. **Want artistic?** → Leonardo AI
3. **Complex concepts?** → DALL-E 3
4. **Versatile needs?** → SDXL

### For Articles:

1. **Need highest quality?** → GPT-4
2. **Complex topics?** → GPT-4 or Claude
3. **Shorter/faster?** → GPT-3.5
4. **SEO important?** → GPT-4

## Quality vs Speed Trade-offs

### Prioritize Quality:

- Use premium models (Veo 3, Imagen 4, GPT-4)
- Longer generation times
- Higher credit costs
- Best results

### Prioritize Speed:

- Use faster models (GPT-3.5, SDXL)
- Faster generation
- Lower credit costs
- Good enough results

### Balanced Approach:

- Start with faster models for iterations
- Use premium models for final content
- Test with faster models first
- Refine with premium models

## Model-Specific Prompting

### Video Models:

- **Kling AI**: Emphasize style and motion
- **Veo 3**: Include technical details (camera, lighting)
- **Runway**: Focus on realistic details
- **Luma**: Specify quality and detail needs

### Image Models:

- **Imagen 4**: Include camera settings and photography terms
- **Leonardo AI**: Use style references and artistic descriptors
- **SDXL**: Be clear about style and composition
- **DALL-E 3**: Describe concepts and abstract ideas

### Language Models:

- **GPT-4**: Can handle complex, detailed prompts
- **GPT-3.5**: Keep prompts simpler and more direct
- **Claude**: Good for analytical and thoughtful content

## Tips for Model Selection

1. **Match Model to Goal**: Choose model that matches your content goals
2. **Test Multiple Models**: Try different models for same prompt
3. **Optimize Prompts**: Adjust prompts for specific model strengths
4. **Consider Trade-offs**: Balance quality, speed, and cost
5. **Learn from Results**: Use successful generations to guide future choices
