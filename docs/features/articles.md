# Articles Feature

Create long-form articles with AI assistance. Generate SEO-optimized content, publish to your blog, and repurpose articles for social media.

## Overview

The Articles feature lets you:

- Generate long-form articles with AI
- Optimize articles for SEO
- Publish articles to your blog
- Repurpose articles for social media
- Track article performance

## Creating Articles

### Generate New Article

**Steps:**

1. Navigate to Studio app
2. Select "Article" content type
3. Enter article topic or prompt
4. Configure article settings
5. Generate article

**Article Settings:**

- **Word Count**: Target length (500-5000 words)
- **Tone**: Professional, casual, friendly, authoritative
- **Target Audience**: Define your audience
- **SEO Keywords**: Include relevant keywords
- **Structure**: Request specific sections

### Article Prompting

**Effective Article Prompts:**

```
Write a 1,500-word article about [topic].
Target audience: [describe audience]
Tone: [professional/casual/friendly]
Structure: Introduction, 5 main points, conclusion
SEO keywords: [keyword1, keyword2, keyword3]
Include: [specific requirements]
```

**Example:**

```
Write a 2,000-word SEO-optimized article about remote work productivity.
Target audience: Remote workers and managers
Tone: Professional yet accessible
Structure: Introduction with hook, 7 productivity tips with subheadings, conclusion
SEO keywords: remote work, productivity tips, work from home, remote team management
Include: Statistics, expert quotes, practical tools
Optimize for: Search engines while maintaining readability
```

### Article Generation Options

**Model Selection:**

- **GPT-4**: Highest quality, best for complex topics
- **GPT-3.5**: Faster generation, good for simpler topics
- **Claude**: Excellent reasoning, detailed analysis

**Quality vs Speed:**

- Use GPT-4 for final content
- Use GPT-3.5 for drafts and iterations
- Test with faster models, refine with premium models
