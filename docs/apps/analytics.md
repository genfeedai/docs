# Analytics App - Complete Guide

Analytics helps you track content performance, measure ROI, analyze engagement, and make data-driven decisions about your content strategy.

## Overview

Analytics provides comprehensive performance tracking across all your platforms. View metrics, track link performance, calculate ROI, compare platforms, and generate insights to optimize your content strategy.

## Getting Started

### Accessing Analytics

1. Navigate to Analytics from main navigation
2. Or use command palette (`⌘K`) and type "analytics"
3. Or use keyboard shortcut `⌘4` (Mac) or `Ctrl+4` (Windows/Linux)

### Analytics Interface

- **Dashboard**: Overview of key metrics
- **Post Analytics**: Detailed post performance
- **Link Tracking**: CTA link performance
- **Platform Comparison**: Compare across platforms
- **Reports**: Generate comprehensive reports

## Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics Explained

**Reach & Impressions:**

- **Reach**: Unique users who saw your content
- **Impressions**: Total number of times content was displayed
- **Difference**: Impressions can be higher if users see content multiple times

**Engagement:**

- **Likes**: User reactions to content
- **Comments**: User interactions and discussions
- **Shares**: Content shared by users
- **Saves**: Content saved for later
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views × 100

**Views & Watch Time:**

- **Views**: Number of times content was viewed
- **Watch Time**: Total time users watched (for videos)
- **Average Watch Time**: Average time per view
- **Completion Rate**: Percentage who watched to end

**Clicks & Conversions:**

- **Clicks**: Clicks on links in content
- **Conversion Rate**: Conversions / Clicks × 100
- **Revenue**: Revenue generated from content
- **ROI**: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100

### Understanding Your Metrics

**High Engagement Rate:**

- Content resonates with audience
- Good time to post
- Content type works well
- Consider creating similar content

**Low Engagement Rate:**

- Content may not match audience
- Posting at wrong time
- Content needs optimization
- Try different content types

**High Views, Low Engagement:**

- Content is discoverable
- Hook works but content doesn't engage
- Improve content quality
- Optimize for engagement

## ROI Calculation

### Understanding ROI

**ROI Formula:**

```
ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
```

**Components:**

- **Revenue**: Money earned from content
- **Cost**: Cost to create content (credits, time)
- **ROI**: Return on investment percentage

### Calculating Content ROI

**Step 1: Track Revenue**

- Revenue from genfeed.ai/u/[handle] links
- Affiliate revenue
- Product sales
- Subscription conversions

**Step 2: Calculate Costs**

- Credits used for generation
- Time investment
- Platform costs
- Other expenses

**Step 3: Calculate ROI**

- Use ROI formula
- Compare across content
- Identify high-ROI content
- Optimize low-ROI content
