Everything in Core, plus organizations, brands, voices & team collaboration.

| Feature | Description |

| **Organizations** | Multi-tenant workspaces for agencies |
| **Brands** | Brand kits, logos, colors, guidelines |
| **Voices & Tones** | Define how your brand sounds |

## Quick Start

1. [**Sign up**](https://genfeed.ai/signup) — Create your account
2. **Create organization** — Your first workspace
3. **Set up brand** — Logo, colors, voice, tone
4. **Invite team** — Add collaborators
5. **Start creating** — Use Studio to generate content

## Core Features

### Organizations

Isolated workspaces for different clients or projects:

- **Multi-tenant** — Each org has its own data
- **Client workspaces** — Invite clients to their org
- **Switch instantly** — Jump between orgs with one click
- **Billing per org** — Separate invoicing if needed

### Brands & Brand Kits

Keep every piece of content on-brand:

- **Brand profiles** — Name, logo, colors, fonts
- **Guidelines** — Do's and don'ts for AI
- **Asset library** — Centralized brand assets
- **Templates** — Pre-approved starting points

### Voices & Tones

Define your brand's personality:

- **Voice profiles** — How your brand speaks (friendly, professional, witty)
- **Tone settings** — Adjust for context (formal email vs casual social)
- **Per-brand voices** — Different voices for different brands
- **Auto-apply** — AI uses your voice in all content

### Team Collaboration

Work together seamlessly:

- **Unlimited seats** — Add your whole team
- **Role-based access** — Admin, editor, viewer
- **Approval workflows** — Review before publish
- **Activity feeds** — See what everyone's working on

### Social Publishing

Publish everywhere from one place:

- Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X
- Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest
- Schedule in advance
- Platform-optimized formatting
