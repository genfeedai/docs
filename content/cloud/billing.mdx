## Which Option is Right for You?

### Choose Subscription if:

- You create content daily
- You want predictable monthly costs
- You need managed infrastructure
- You prefer automatic updates and support

### Choose Pay-As-You-Go if:

- You have variable content needs
- You want to control spending precisely
- You're evaluating the platform
- You need flexibility in usage

### Choose Self-Hosted if:

- You have technical resources
- You want full control over infrastructure
- You have your own AI API keys
- You prefer open-source solutions
